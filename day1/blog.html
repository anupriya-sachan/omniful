Your Guide to: Homemade Xiao Long Bao

Written by Ashley

July 10, 2025

Oh, Xiao Long Bao. Just the name conjures images of delicate, pleated parcels, each holding a secret treasure of savory broth and succulent pork. It's the kind of dish that feels like pure magic â€“ a burst of hot, umami-rich soup followed by tender meat, all encased in a perfectly thin, chewy wrapper. For years, I thought these ethereal soup dumplings were strictly the domain of seasoned dim sum chefs. The idea of making them at home felt as daunting as scaling Mount Everest with a pair of chopsticks. But, my fellow food adventurers, I'm here to tell you: it's absolutely achievable! Yes, it requires a little patience, a dash of precision, and a whole lot of love, but the reward? A truly unforgettable culinary experience that will impress anyone lucky enough to share a bite. So, roll up your sleeves, put on your favorite cooking playlist, and let's unravel the delicious mystery of homemade Xiao Long Bao. Get ready to create your own pockets of pure joy!

What you'll need

  • 1 large Yellow Onion, quartered
  • 2-3 slices Fresh Ginger
  • 2-3 Green Onions, roughly chopped
  • 8 cups Water
  • 1 tbsp Shaoxing Wine (or dry sherry)
  • 1 tsp Salt
